Lionel Messi

The little boy from rosario

Loyal to his club, FC Barcelona, and his country Argentina. Messi has won every major trophy the sport has to offer. Including The World Cup.

Here's a time line of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina
  • 2004 - Joined FC Barcelona
  • 2008 - Helped Barcelona achieve their first treble
  • 2009 - Won his first Ballon D'or
  • 2011 - This season, Lionel Messi scored the most goals ever for a calender year season
  • 2015 - Won the treble with Barca, and was apart of arguably the greatest trio of all time, known as MSN
  • 2021 - Won his 7th Ballon D'or, the most in history
  • 2022 - Won the World Cup for Argentina, the last trophie to cement him as the greatest to ever play the game
